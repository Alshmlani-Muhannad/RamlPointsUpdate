Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about converting loyalty points with RamlPoints

We currently support 6 major loyalty programs: Al Rajhi Bank (0.00199 SAR per point), Qitaf (0.08 SAR per point), Akther Alinma (0.0014 SAR per point), ANB Rewards (0.005 SAR per point), IZ Wallet Program (0.0016 SAR per point), and BSF Jana Points (0.004 SAR per point). Each program has different conversion rates and processing methods.

Processing times vary by program: Al Rajhi, Qitaf, Akther Alinma, and ANB Rewards (2-4 days via OTP process), IZ Wallet Program and BSF Jana Points (3-5 days via phone coordination). You'll receive WhatsApp updates throughout the process.

We charge a standard 5% processing fee across all programs. This covers secure processing, bank transfers, and customer support. The fee is deducted from your conversion amount before transfer.

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and are part of the regulated RamlGroup ecosystem. Your data is protected with the same security standards used by major Saudi financial institutions.

With a 99.8% success rate, failures are rare. If a conversion fails due to account issues or insufficient points, we'll notify you immediately via WhatsApp and provide full support to resolve the issue.

Minimum requirements vary by program: Al Rajhi (5,000 points), Qitaf (100 points), Akther Alinma (7,000 points), ANB Rewards (2,000 points), IZ Wallet Program (6,000 points), and BSF Jana Points (2,500 points). These minimums ensure cost-effective processing.

We provide real-time updates via WhatsApp throughout the entire process. You'll receive notifications when we receive your request, when processing begins, and when the cash transfer is completed to your account.

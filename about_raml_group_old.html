Our Story

Founded in 2018 with a vision to revolutionize how Saudi consumers interact with their financial assets, RamlGroup emerged from a simple observation: millions of loyalty points were going unused across the Kingdom.

What started as a small team of fintech enthusiasts has grown into a comprehensive financial technology ecosystem, serving over 50,000 customers and processing millions in value recovery through innovative solutions.

Today, RamlGroup stands as a trusted name in Saudi fintech, combining traditional financial wisdom with cutting-edge technology to create value where none existed before.

Our Mission

To unlock hidden financial value for every Saudi consumer through innovative, secure, and transparent fintech solutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading financial optimization platform in the Middle East, empowering millions to maximize their financial potential.
